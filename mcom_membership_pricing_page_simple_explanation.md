# MCOM Membership Pricing Page (Simple Explanation)

---

## 1. Top Section (First Thing People See)

This section should be very clear and simple.

Headline:
Join the MCOM Business Network

Subheadline:
Get visibility, tools, and support to grow your business

---

## 2. Show the 4 Membership Levels

There should be 4 main options:

- Bronze (Starter)
- Silver (Growing)
- Gold (Scaling)
- Platinum (Premium)

Each one should be shown in a box (card).

---

## 3. Basic View of Each Plan

Each plan should show:

Name
Price (starting from)
Short description
Key features
Button

---

### Bronze

From £10/month

- Basic business listing
- Low visibility
- Community access

Button: Choose Bronze

---

### Silver

From £75/month

- Everything in Bronze
- Marketing tools
- Campaign participation

Button: Choose Silver

---

### Gold (Highlight This One)

From £350/month

- Everything in Silver
- Full campaign access
- Advanced tools
- Multi-location support

Button: Choose Gold

---

### Platinum

From £1200/month

- Everything in Gold
- Priority visibility
- Premium access
- Dedicated support

Button: Choose Platinum

---

## 4. Explain the Levels (Normal, Pro, Pro+)

Each membership has 3 levels:

- Normal
- Pro
- Pro+

Explain it simply:

Normal:

- Basic access

Pro:

- More tools and growth features

Pro+:

- More visibility and priority

---

## 5. Show Full Pricing Inside Each Plan

When a user clicks a plan, show more details.

Example (Bronze):

Bronze – £10/month

- Basic listing

Bronze Pro – £25/month

- Better visibility
- More insights

Bronze Pro+ – £50/month

- Visibility boost
- More tools

Do the same for:

- Silver
- Gold
- Platinum

---

## 6. Comparison Table

Add a simple table to compare plans.

Example:

- Visibility (low → high)
- Campaign access (none → full)
- Tools (basic → advanced)
- Support (basic → dedicated)

This helps users decide faster.

---

## 7. Who Each Plan Is For

Explain simply:

- Bronze → New businesses
- Silver → Growing businesses
- Gold → Scaling businesses
- Platinum → Established businesses

---

## 8. Trust Section

Add simple trust points:

- Built for real businesses
- Easy to upgrade anytime
- Designed to grow with you

---

## 9. Final Section (Call To Action)

Headline:
Start Growing Your Business Today

Text:
Choose your membership and get started in minutes

Buttons:

- Get Started
- Compare Plans

---

## 10. Important Rules for Developers

- Keep the page clean and simple
- Highlight Gold as the most popular plan
- Make buttons clear and easy to click
- Allow users to:
  - Select a plan
  - Choose Normal, Pro, or Pro+
  - Go to checkout

Optional:

- Add Monthly / Yearly toggle
- Add upgrade option later

---

## Final Summary

This page should make it easy for a business owner to:

1. Understand what membership is
2. See the difference between plans
3. Choose a plan quickly
4. Sign up without confusion

That’s the goal.
