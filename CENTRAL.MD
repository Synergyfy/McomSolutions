Based on this meeting, and aligning with Henry's explanations, **MCOMSolutions (or MCOM Central)** is not another business platform like MCOM Rewards, MCOM Spin, MCOM Mall, Audit, or Expo.

Its primary purpose is:

> "The one place that controls authentication, registration, membership, packages, subscriptions, pricing, payments, permissions and launches users into the platform they want to use."

Therefore, the front-facing side is not a normal marketplace or loyalty platform.

It is essentially the:

* Entry Point
* Identity Layer
* Subscription Layer
* Membership Layer
* Platform Launcher
* User Command Centre

for the entire MCOM Ecosystem.

# HENRY'S FRONT-FACING VISION IN ONE SENTENCE

MCOMSolutions is not a business platform itself.

It is the **single ecosystem gateway that allows businesses, customers, partners, agents, consultants and account managers to register once, subscribe once, manage memberships and packages, maintain one profile, and launch any MCOM platform without having to sign in again.**

Everything Henry described in this meeting ultimately revolves around these five front-facing pillars:

1. **Central Registration**
2. **Central Authentication**
3. **Central Membership & Pricing**
4. **Central Platform Launcher**
5. **Central Business Profile & Permissions Management**

Those are the core user-facing functions of MCOMSolutions.
